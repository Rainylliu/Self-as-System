Dream of the 4th Day

It felt like I was watching an immersive
movie where I wasn't the main character,
but in the first person perspective of the
main character. The protagonist is a
rogue thief tasked with infiltrating a
heavily guarded private exhibition held
by the Mafia. His goal was to retrieve a
beautifully crafted vase.

In the beginning, the protagonist and
two teammates skillfully lure two guards
at the basement entrance. After all is
well, the protagonist enters the exhibition
alone and begins to search for the
precious vase. The entire exhibition is
filled with sculptures, all made of pale
pink ceramics decorated with
shimmering, crystal-like shards that
glinter in the light.

When he finally found the vase and held
it in his hand to examine it, the alarm
suddenly sounded. What follows is a
heated chase between the protagonist
and the building's security team. He was
preparing to escape, but just as freedom
seemed within reach, he hearda familiar
voice. As he turned around, he was
knocked down from behind and passed
out. At that moment, I woke up.

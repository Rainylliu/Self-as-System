" Mood of All Day "

fear

Worried about water damage to my computer, I rushed out the door, filled with anxiety and worry that my computer might be beyond repair.

pensiveness

At the repair store, staff said liquid damage was complicated and unlikely to be fixed. Disappointed, I carried the computer home, feeling powerless and thinking about possible solutions.

sadness

After lunch, my body was very tired and I lay down on my bed and fell asleep again without realizing it. I felt physically and mentally exhausted from the whole day's exhaustion and emotional stress.

acceptance

In the evening, my friend advised turning the computer over in a ventilated spot. Their comfort helped me relax, gradually easing the day's fatigue.

" Mood of All Day "

fear

In the morning, I felt nervous and uneasy about the upcoming exams and presentations. Despite preparing, the fear of unknown factors left me worried about handling the challenges ahead.

angry

At noon, I felt irritated and angry when the subway stopped due to an accident. Being prepared but possibly late due to traffic issues stressed me and disrupted my plans.

joy

In the afternoon, I finished the exam and presentation, and all the stress vanished. I felt relaxed, happy, and excited, relieved that everything was completed successfully and back to normal.

acceptance

At night, I listened to soft music before bed, feeling peaceful and content. Reflecting on the day's experiences, I relaxed and gradually drifted into sleep with the music.
